# 002 — Customer discovery (3 interviews, required)

PULSO requires **≥3 customer-discovery interviews**, recorded, submitted on an accessible drive link. It is also a full judging criterion. Most teams flake here — this is where Lendoor wins, because we have real users and a real B2B pipeline.

## Who to interview (pick 3, aim for variety)
1. **A credit-invisible user** — someone who uses a crypto wallet (Bitso/Lemon) but can't get credit from a bank. The actual end user.
2. **A wallet/fintech operator** — a PM or founder at a LatAm wallet/exchange (ideally Bitso-adjacent) who would embed a credit layer. The B2B buyer.
3. **An existing Lendoor borrower** — someone already using the live product, to validate the repayment/credit-ladder behavior.

## What to ask (validate the problem, not pitch)
- How do you get credit today? What happens when you need money short-term?
- Have you ever been rejected for credit? Why do you think?
- Do you hold/use digital dollars (USDC)? Through which app?
- (Operator) Would you embed an uncollateralized credit product in your wallet? What stops you today?
- How do you solve this problem right now (the workaround)?

## Logistics
- 15-20 min each, record (Zoom/Meet/phone with consent).
- Upload recordings to a Drive folder, set link to "anyone with the link can view".
- Keep a 3-5 bullet summary per interview for the deck (who, problem today, key quote).

## Timing
Do these **in parallel** with the build, not at the end. Lucho's existing pipeline (wallet/fintech contacts + live borrowers) makes 3 interviews a 1-2 day task. Start day 1-2.
